Hazelnut Cream

Smooth, nutty luxury - like Nutella without the guilt

Calories 285
Protein 44g
Carbs 22g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 400 ml reduced fat high protein milk (A2 Protein Plus or Devondale Protein Plus)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 30 g hazelnut butter or hazelnut meal
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 2 ml hazelnut extract (Queen brand - essential!)
  • 22 g vanilla whey protein
  • 35 g erythritol or Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.8 g xanthan gum

Instructions

  1. Add milk, egg yolk, hazelnut butter/meal, vanilla, and hazelnut extract to container
  2. Blend with immersion blender for 90 seconds (hazelnuts must fully incorporate)
  3. Mix dry ingredients in separate bowl
  4. Continue blending while slowly adding dry ingredients
  5. Mix additional 90 seconds after all ingredients added
  6. Let sit 10 minutes for foam to settle
  7. Pour into pint, cap and freeze 24 hours
  8. Run outside of pint under hot water for 60 seconds before spinning
  9. Spin on Light Ice Cream setting

Nutrition per Pint

285 Calories
44g Protein
22g Carbs
8g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Hazelnut extract is KEY - without it, flavor is too subtle
  • Optional Roasted Hazelnut Topping: 15g hazelnuts, roughly chopped, toast at 160°C for 8-10 minutes, cool completely, freeze solid, sprinkle over ice cream before eating (adds ~95 calories)
  • If using hazelnut meal, strain mixture to remove any chunks
  • Toast hazelnuts for topping just before adding (maximum flavor)
  • Alternative: Use Nutella (15g) for stronger flavor (+80 cal)
  • Adelaide Shopping: Hazelnut butter at Woolworths, health food stores, or Bulk Nutrients. Hazelnut extract (Queen brand) at Coles/Woolworths. Hazelnuts at Coles/Woolworths (buy raw, roast yourself)
