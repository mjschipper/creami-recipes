Grapefruit Sorbet

Sophisticated and refreshing - bright citrus flavour

Calories 95
Protein 1g
Carbs 22g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 220g fresh grapefruit juice (with pulp)
  • 220g water
  • 50g erythritol/Natvia (highest - grapefruit is tart)
  • 0.5g salt (1/8 tsp)
  • 1/2 grapefruit, zested

Instructions

  1. Zest half grapefruit with microplane into container
  2. Cut grapefruit in half
  3. Squeeze juice into bowl, scrape remaining pulp
  4. CRITICAL: Avoid white pith - extremely bitter
  5. Pour 220g grapefruit juice through strainer
  6. Add pulp from strainer (discard seeds)
  7. Add 220g water, erythritol and salt
  8. Blend with immersion blender 90 seconds
  9. Pour into pint, freeze 24 hours
  10. Rinse, spin, re-spin 1-2 times

Nutrition per Pint

95 Calories
1g Protein
22g Carbs
0g Fat

Pro Tips

  • The zest is essential for authentic grapefruit flavour
  • Mixture may separate into layers - creates interesting contrast
