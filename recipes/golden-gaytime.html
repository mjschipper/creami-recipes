Golden Gaytime Ice Cream

Iconic Australian ice cream bar flavour

Calories 310
Protein 44g
Carbs 30g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 400 ml 2% high protein milk
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 10 g caramel extract or sugar-free caramel syrup
  • 16 g vanilla whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 10 ml fat-free milk powder
  • 25 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.7 g xanthan gum
  • 20 g crushed biscuit crumbs (Arnott's Scotch Finger)
  • 15 g chocolate coating pieces

Instructions

  1. Add milk, egg yolk, vanilla, and caramel to high-sided container
  2. Mix protein, milk powder, erythritol, salt, xanthan gum in bowl
  3. Blend wet ingredients with immersion blender
  4. Slowly add dry ingredients while blending
  5. Mix additional 90 seconds
  6. Let sit 10 minutes, pour into pint, freeze 24 hours
  7. Prepare biscuit crumb mixture: crush biscuits, mix with chopped chocolate
  8. Freeze crumb mixture on parchment paper
  9. Hot water rinse 60 seconds
  10. Spin on light ice cream setting
  11. Make hole in center, add frozen crumb mixture
  12. Cover with ice cream, run mix-in setting
  13. Top with extra crumbs

Nutrition per Pint

310 Calories
44g Protein
30g Carbs
8g Fat

Pro Tips

  • The toffee-vanilla base with biscuit crumbs captures the Gaytime magic
  • Use Arnott's Scotch Finger or Nice biscuits for authentic crumb texture
