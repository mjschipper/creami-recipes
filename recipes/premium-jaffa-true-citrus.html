Premium Jaffa with Chocolate Coating (True Citrus)

Restaurant-quality Jaffa with melted chocolate and chocolate chip mix-in using True Orange packets

Calories 320
Protein 50g
Carbs 26g
Freeze Time 36 hours

Ingredients

  • 420 ml high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 2 packets True Orange (True Citrus Orange packets)
  • 32 g chocolate protein powder
  • 10 g cocoa powder (Cadbury Bournville recommended)
  • 40 g erythritol or Natvia
  • 1 egg yolk (extra creaminess)
  • 14 g melted dark chocolate (70% cacao - Haigh's or Lindt)
  • 1 g xanthan gum
  • 1 g salt
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 28 g dark chocolate chips (frozen solid, for mix-in)

Instructions

  1. Add milk and vanilla to high-sided container
  2. Dissolve True Orange packets in the milk (stir well to ensure crystals fully dissolve)
  3. Melt dark chocolate (microwave 30 seconds or double boiler)
  4. Whisk melted chocolate and egg yolk into milk mixture
  5. Mix all dry ingredients in separate bowl
  6. Blend milk/chocolate mixture with immersion blender
  7. Slowly add dry ingredients while blending
  8. Continue mixing 90 seconds after everything's incorporated
  9. Let sit 10 minutes for foam to settle
  10. Pour into pint, cap and freeze 36 hours (heated chocolate needs longer freeze time)
  11. Run outside of pint under hot water for 60 seconds before spinning
  12. Spin on Sorbet setting
  13. Make hole in corner with spoon, add frozen chocolate chips
  14. Run Mix-In setting immediately

Nutrition per Pint

320 Calories
50g Protein
26g Carbs
9g Fat

Pro Tips

  • True Orange packets: Available at iHerb.com.au (best selection), Amazon.com.au, some Chemist Warehouse locations. Provides natural orange flavor without the need for fresh zest or extract
  • Chocolate Quality Matters: Haigh's (Rundle Mall, Adelaide) - support local! Lindt 70% dark (Coles/Woolworths) or Callebaut (Essential Ingredient, 78 Hutt St)
  • Freeze Chocolate Chips: Freeze chips solid before adding - prevents them from melting during mix-in
  • Egg Yolk Magic: The egg yolk adds serious creaminess - like gelato!
  • 36 Hour Freeze: Heated chocolate needs longer freeze time - don't rush it!
  • True Citrus Advantage: True Orange packets provide consistent, natural orange flavor without needing fresh oranges. No zesting required!
  • For Authentic Jaffa Candy Pieces: Buy actual Jaffas, freeze them solid, chop into quarters, and add as mix-in for ultimate nostalgia
  • This is genuinely restaurant-quality Jaffa flavor - the premium version is worth the extra calories!
  • Comparison to Original: This version uses True Orange packets instead of orange extract and zest, providing a more consistent and convenient orange flavor
