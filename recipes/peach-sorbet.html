Peach Sorbet

Personal favourite - perfectly peachy

Calories 105
Protein 1g
Carbs 26g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 300 g fresh peaches (pitted, skin on)
  • 140 ml water
  • 30 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt

Instructions

  1. Rinse peaches thoroughly
  2. Cut around middle following natural seam
  3. Twist halves in opposite directions
  4. Remove pit, keep skin on (adds colour and nutrients)
  5. Add 300g pitted peaches to high-sided container
  6. Add 140g water, erythritol and salt
  7. Use immersion blender to completely crush
  8. Continue blending 90 seconds until completely smooth
  9. Pour into pint, cap, freeze 24 hours
  10. Hot water rinse 60 seconds
  11. Spin on Sorbet, re-spin 2-3 times

Nutrition per Pint

105 Calories
1g Protein
26g Carbs
0g Fat

Pro Tips

  • White peaches are preferred - sweeter, more delicate
  • Best in peak season (December-February in Australia)
