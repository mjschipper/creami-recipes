Lamington Ice Cream

Queensland invention, national icon - chocolate coconut delight

Calories 290
Protein 42g
Carbs 24g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 400 ml light high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 16 g chocolate whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 8 g cocoa powder (2 tbsp)
  • 25 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.7 g xanthan gum
  • 25 g desiccated coconut
  • 15 g strawberry jam (optional, for raspberry lamington)

Instructions

  1. Add milk, egg yolk, and vanilla to high-sided container
  2. Mix protein, cocoa, erythritol, salt, xanthan gum in bowl
  3. Blend wet ingredients with immersion blender
  4. Slowly add dry ingredients while blending
  5. Mix additional 90 seconds
  6. Let sit 10 minutes, pour into pint, freeze 24 hours
  7. Toast 15g coconut in dry pan until golden (watch carefully!)
  8. Freeze toasted and remaining raw coconut separately
  9. Hot water rinse 60 seconds
  10. Spin on light ice cream setting
  11. Make hole in center, add frozen coconut
  12. If using jam, drizzle through for raspberry lamington effect
  13. Cover with ice cream, run mix-in setting
  14. Top with extra toasted coconut

Nutrition per Pint

290 Calories
42g Protein
24g Carbs
9g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Toasting some coconut adds depth and authenticity
  • For raspberry lamington, swirl through sugar-free strawberry jam
