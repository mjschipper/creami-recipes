Simple Chocolate Ice Cream

Basic chocolate ice cream - light and satisfying

Calories 175
Protein 16g
Carbs 20g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 160g 1% high protein milk
  • 280g unsweetened almond milk
  • 2.1g vanilla extract (1/2 tsp)
  • 50g erythritol/Natvia
  • 8g cocoa powder (2 tbsp)
  • 1g salt (1/4 tsp)
  • 1g xanthan gum (1/4 tsp)

Instructions

  1. Combine milks and vanilla in high-sided container
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl
  3. Blend with immersion blender while adding dry ingredients
  4. Mix 90 seconds after all ingredients added
  5. Freeze 24 hours
  6. Rinse with hot water, spin on light ice cream
  7. Re-spin or mix-in for consistency

Nutrition per Pint

175 Calories
16g Protein
20g Carbs
3g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Use high-quality cocoa powder like Cadbury Bournville for best flavour
