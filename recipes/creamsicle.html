Creamsicle Ice Cream

Classic orange creamsicle flavor using True Citrus Orange - childhood nostalgia!

Calories 185
Protein 28g
Carbs 20g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 440 ml light high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 5 ml (1 tsp) vanilla extract
  • 2 packets True Orange (or True Citrus Orange)
  • 16 g (~½ scoop) vanilla whey protein powder
  • 10 g (2 tbsp) skim milk powder
  • 30 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 1 g (¼ tsp) salt
  • 0.7 g (scant ¼ tsp) xanthan gum

Instructions

  1. Add milk and vanilla to high-sided container
  2. Dissolve True Orange packets in the milk (stir well)
  3. Mix all dry ingredients in separate bowl
  4. Blend wet ingredients with immersion blender
  5. Slowly add dry ingredients while blending
  6. Mix additional 90 seconds after all ingredients added
  7. Taste - should be sweeter and more orange than desired (frozen foods taste 30% less sweet)
  8. Pour into pint, cap, freeze 24 hours
  9. Hot water rinse 60 seconds before spinning
  10. Process on Light Ice Cream setting
  11. Re-spin if needed for desired consistency

Pro Tips

  • True Citrus is KEY: Provides concentrated orange flavor without adding liquid that would affect texture
  • Start with 2 packets: Can reduce to 1 if you prefer subtle orange, or increase to 3 for intense flavor
  • Taste before freezing: Base should taste VERY orange - frozen it will be much milder
  • Vanilla is essential: Balances the citrus and creates authentic creamsicle flavor
  • Cost per packet: ~$0.20 per True Orange packet, so this recipe adds about $0.40 to base cost
