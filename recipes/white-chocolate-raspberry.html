White Chocolate Raspberry Ice Cream

Fruity elegance with white chocolate richness

Calories 310
Protein 44g
Carbs 26g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 380 ml 2% high protein milk
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 20 g white chocolate, melted (use real white chocolate - Lindt, Haigh's)
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 22 g vanilla whey protein
  • 30 g erythritol or Natvia (less because white chocolate adds sweetness)
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.8 g xanthan gum
  • 80 g fresh or frozen raspberries
  • 10 g erythritol (for raspberry swirl)
  • 5 ml lemon juice
  • 15 g white chocolate, chopped into small chunks and frozen (optional)

Instructions

  1. Make raspberry swirl first: Combine raspberries, 10g erythritol, lemon juice in bowl, microwave 90 seconds, mash with fork, strain through fine mesh (IMPORTANT - seeds will be annoying), cool completely, freeze until semi-solid (jam-like consistency)
  2. Warm milk to 27-32°C (80-90°F) - NOT boiling!
  3. Melt 20g white chocolate (microwave 20 seconds, stir, repeat)
  4. Add milk, egg yolk, melted white chocolate, vanilla to container
  5. Blend with immersion blender for 60 seconds
  6. Mix dry ingredients separately
  7. Continue blending while adding dry ingredients
  8. Mix 90 seconds after all ingredients added
  9. Let sit 10 minutes for foam to settle
  10. Pour into pint, cap and freeze 24 hours
  11. Run outside of pint under hot water for 60 seconds before spinning
  12. Spin on Light Ice Cream setting
  13. Make 2-3 small holes throughout pint, use spoon to place raspberry swirl in ribbons (don't mix fully - want visible pink swirls!), add white chocolate chunks if using, run Mix-in setting very briefly (5 seconds) - just to distribute, not mix

Nutrition per Pint

310 Calories
44g Protein
26g Carbs
8g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Must use REAL white chocolate (cocoa butter) - not white "melts" or "coating chocolate"
  • Straining raspberry seeds is worth the effort
  • Don't fully mix raspberry swirl - want visible ribbons
  • White chocolate tastes sweeter when frozen - reduce erythritol
  • Adelaide Shopping: White chocolate (Lindt, Haigh's at 78 Hutt St, or Cadbury Dream) at Coles/Woolworths. Raspberries: Fresh from Central Market (best flavor) or frozen from Coles/Woolworths
  • Warning: White chocolate chips don't exist sugar-free in Australia - use chunks
  • Variations: White Chocolate Strawberry (use 100g strawberries), White Chocolate Blueberry (use 100g blueberries), White Chocolate Lemon (skip berries, add 10g lemon juice + lemon zest)
