Strawberry Sorbet

Classic crowd-pleaser with almost ice cream-like texture

Calories 100
Protein 1g
Carbs 24g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 300g fresh strawberries
  • 130g water
  • 20g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5g salt (1/8 tsp)

Instructions

  1. Rinse strawberries, remove stems
  2. Cut larger berries in half
  3. Add strawberries directly to pint up to 300g
  4. Add 130g water, erythritol and salt
  5. Use immersion blender IN pint for 60-90 seconds
  6. Blend until completely smooth
  7. Cap and freeze 24 hours
  8. Hot water rinse 60 seconds
  9. Spin on Sorbet setting
  10. After first spin, may look nearly done (pectin adds body)
  11. Re-spin once more for perfect consistency

Nutrition per Pint

100 Calories
1g Protein
24g Carbs
0g Fat

Pro Tips

  • The natural pectin in strawberries creates incredible body
  • Can puree directly in the pint - saves dishes!
