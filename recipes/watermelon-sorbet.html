Watermelon Sorbet

Summer refreshment champion - incredibly low calorie

Calories 100
Protein 0g
Carbs 25g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 330 ml fresh watermelon juice/puree
  • 120 ml water
  • 20 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt

Instructions

  1. Choose watermelon with deep yellow-orange field spot
  2. Cut flesh into chunks, removing all white rind
  3. Blend in food processor for 30 seconds
  4. Add 330g watermelon juice to pint
  5. Add 120g water
  6. Add erythritol and salt
  7. Blend with immersion blender for 30 seconds
  8. Taste - should be sweeter than desired
  9. Cap and freeze 24 hours
  10. Hot water rinse for 60 seconds
  11. Spin on Sorbet setting
  12. Re-spin 2 times, leveling between spins

Nutrition per Pint

100 Calories
0g Protein
25g Carbs
0g Fat

Pro Tips

  • One large watermelon makes ~15 pints
  • Store extra juice in containers in fridge for up to 3 days
  • Cost: $0.27-0.33 per pint!
