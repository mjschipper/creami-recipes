All-Milk Chocolate Ice Cream

Noticeably creamier than the almond milk version

Calories 190
Protein 28g
Carbs 18g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 440 ml light high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 2.1 g vanilla extract
  • 50 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 8 g cocoa powder (2 tbsp)
  • 1 g salt
  • 1 g xanthan gum

Instructions

  1. Add milk and vanilla to high-sided container
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl
  3. Blend with immersion blender while adding dry ingredients
  4. Mix 90 seconds after all ingredients added
  5. Freeze 24 hours
  6. Process as standard

Nutrition per Pint

190 Calories
28g Protein
18g Carbs
3g Fat

Pro Tips

  • All-dairy base produces superior texture compared to almond milk version
