Indulgent Cookies & Cream

Loaded with Oreos - no guilt needed on special occasions

Calories 580
Protein 48g
Carbs 52g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 280 ml reduced fat high protein milk (A2 Protein Plus or Devondale Protein Plus)
  • 100 ml heavy cream
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 22 g vanilla whey protein (2/3 scoop)
  • 30 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 20 g real sugar
  • 0.8 g xanthan gum
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 60 g Oreos, crushed
  • 20 g additional Oreos for topping

Instructions

  1. Make rich vanilla base with cream and 2 egg yolks
  2. Freeze 24 hours
  3. Spin on light ice cream
  4. Add 60g crushed Oreos as mix-in
  5. Top with additional Oreos
  6. This tastes like actual Oreo ice cream, not diet ice cream

Nutrition per Pint

580 Calories
48g Protein
52g Carbs
22g Fat

Pro Tips
