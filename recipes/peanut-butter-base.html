Peanut Butter Ice Cream

Smooth, rich peanut butter flavour in every bite

Calories 320
Protein 52g
Carbs 18g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 380 ml light high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 45 ml natural peanut butter (creamy)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 22 g vanilla whey protein (2/3 scoop)
  • 30 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.8 g xanthan gum

Instructions

  1. Add milk, peanut butter, egg yolk, vanilla to container
  2. Blend with immersion blender for 90 seconds - PB must fully emulsify
  3. Mixture should be completely smooth with no oil separation
  4. Mix dry ingredients in bowl
  5. Continue blending while adding dry ingredients
  6. Mix additional 90 seconds
  7. Let sit 10 minutes
  8. Pour into pint and freeze 24 hours
  9. Hot water rinse, spin on light ice cream
  10. Re-spin for smooth consistency

Nutrition per Pint

320 Calories
52g Protein
18g Carbs
12g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Use natural PB (just peanuts + salt) - not Skippy/Jif
  • Warm PB slightly in microwave (10 sec) for easier blending
  • Variations: PB&J (add jam), PB Chocolate (add cocoa), PB Fudge (add chips)
