Cinnamon Cream Pie Ice Cream

Warm cinnamon spice with cookie pieces

Calories 270
Protein 48g
Carbs 24g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 440g fat-free high protein milk
  • 20g brown sugar erythritol
  • 4.2g vanilla extract (1 tsp)
  • 20g vanilla whey protein (3/4 scoop)
  • 20g fat-free milk powder
  • 8g cinnamon (1 tbsp)
  • 0.5g salt (1/8 tsp)
  • 1g xanthan gum (1/4 tsp)
  • 1.3 Biscoff cookies (1 for mix-in, 1/3 for topping)

Instructions

  1. Mix base using immersion blender method
  2. Freeze Biscoff cookie next to pint for 24 hours
  3. Hot water rinse pint
  4. Spin on light ice cream setting
  5. Make thin hole near SIDE of pint
  6. Fit frozen cookie into hole
  7. Cover with ice cream, run mix-in setting
  8. Chop 1/3 additional cookie, top pint with chunks

Nutrition per Pint

270 Calories
48g Protein
24g Carbs
4g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Freezing the cookie prevents complete pulverization
