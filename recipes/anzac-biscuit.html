Anzac Biscuit Ice Cream

WWI heritage biscuit - oats, coconut, golden syrup

Calories 320
Protein 40g
Carbs 32g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 400g 2% high protein milk
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5g vanilla extract (1 tsp)
  • 10g golden syrup
  • 16g vanilla whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 10g fat-free milk powder
  • 25g erythritol brown sugar
  • 0.5g salt (1/8 tsp)
  • 0.7g xanthan gum
  • 20g rolled oats, toasted
  • 15g desiccated coconut, toasted
  • 2 Arnott's Anzac biscuits, frozen and crushed

Instructions

  1. Toast oats and coconut separately in dry pan until golden
  2. Let cool completely, then freeze
  3. Add milk, egg yolk, vanilla, and golden syrup to container
  4. Mix protein, milk powder, erythritol, salt, xanthan gum in bowl
  5. Blend wet ingredients with immersion blender
  6. Slowly add dry ingredients while blending
  7. Mix additional 90 seconds
  8. Let sit 10 minutes, pour into pint, freeze 24 hours
  9. Freeze Anzac biscuits until solid, crush into chunks
  10. Hot water rinse 60 seconds
  11. Spin on light ice cream setting
  12. Make hole in center
  13. Add frozen toasted oats, coconut, and biscuit pieces
  14. Cover with ice cream, run mix-in setting
  15. Top with extra oats and coconut

Nutrition per Pint

320 Calories
40g Protein
32g Carbs
10g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Toasting the oats and coconut is essential for authentic flavour
  • The golden syrup in the base captures the Anzac biscuit essence
