White Chocolate Caramel Ice Cream

Luxurious white chocolate with buttery caramel ribbons

Calories 330
Protein 45g
Carbs 28g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 380 ml reduced fat high protein milk (A2 Protein Plus or Devondale Protein Plus)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 20 g white chocolate, melted (use real white chocolate - Lindt, Haigh's, or Cadbury Dream)
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 22 g vanilla whey protein
  • 25 g erythritol or Natvia (reduced because white chocolate is sweet)
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.8 g xanthan gum
  • 30 g sugar-free caramel sauce (freeze until semi-solid)

Instructions

  1. Warm milk to 27-32°C (80-90°F) - just warm, NOT hot
  2. Melt 20g white chocolate (microwave 15 seconds, stir, repeat until just melted)
  3. Add milk, egg yolk, melted white chocolate, vanilla to container
  4. Blend with immersion blender for 60 seconds
  5. Mix dry ingredients in separate bowl
  6. Continue blending while slowly adding dry ingredients
  7. Mix additional 90 seconds after all ingredients added
  8. Let sit 10 minutes for foam to settle
  9. Pour into pint, cap and freeze 24 hours
  10. Prepare caramel: Make sugar-free caramel sauce (see Salted Caramel recipe), freeze 30g portion until semi-solid (jam-like consistency) - about 2 hours OR use Walden Farms caramel syrup straight from bottle
  11. Run outside of pint under hot water for 60 seconds before spinning
  12. Spin on Light Ice Cream setting
  13. Make 3-4 small holes throughout pint (not just center)
  14. Use spoon to place semi-solid caramel in ribbons through holes
  15. Don't fully mix - you want visible golden caramel swirls
  16. Run Mix-in setting very briefly (5 seconds) - just to distribute slightly
  17. Should see distinct amber caramel ribbons throughout white chocolate base

Nutrition per Pint

330 Calories
45g Protein
28g Carbs
9g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Must use REAL white chocolate with cocoa butter - not white "melts" or coating chocolate
  • White chocolate is sensitive to heat - don't overheat or it will seize
  • Caramel swirl should be thick (semi-solid) so it creates ribbons, not mix completely
  • White chocolate tastes sweeter when frozen - that's why we use less erythritol
  • If caramel is too liquid, it will just mix in and disappear
  • Adelaide Shopping: White chocolate (Haigh's at 78 Hutt St - local!, Lindt, or Cadbury Dream) at Coles/Woolworths. Caramel sauce: Make your own or Walden Farms online (iHerb, Amazon AU). Alternative: Queen Caramel Extract (5ml) mixed with 10g heavy cream for caramel flavor
  • Variations: Salted White Chocolate Caramel (increase salt to 1g), White Chocolate Caramel Crunch (add 15g toasted pecans or walnuts as mix-in - adds ~100 cal), White Chocolate Butterscotch (use butterscotch extract instead of caramel swirl), Premium Version (use 30g real white chocolate - adds ~50 cal for ultra-creamy)
  • CRITICAL: The 36-hour freeze rule applies here because you heated the milk. Work quickly during caramel swirling - ice cream melts fast. Don't over-mix or caramel will disappear into base
  • Comparison to Shop Version: Shop ice cream likely 800-1,200+ calories per pint. Your version: 330 calories. Savings: 470-870 calories while maintaining indulgent flavor!
