Zero Sugar Jell-O Pudding Base

Ultra-thick and creamy using sugar-free instant pudding mix

Calories 175
Protein 15g
Carbs 22g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 240 g 1% high protein milk
  • 200 g unsweetened almond milk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 35 g erythritol or Natvia (reduce if your pudding is sweet)
  • 7 g sugar-free instant pudding mix (e.g. Jell-O, any flavour)
  • 0.5 g xanthan gum (optional, for extra body)
  • 1 g salt

Instructions

  1. Add milk, almond milk and vanilla to a high-sided container
  2. In a bowl, whisk together erythritol, pudding mix, xanthan gum (if using) and salt
  3. Blend the liquid, then slowly rain in the dry mix while blending
  4. Blend an additional 90 seconds after everything is added
  5. Let sit 5–10 minutes for foam to settle, then pour into pint
  6. Cap pint and freeze 24 hours minimum
  7. Run outside of pint under hot water for 60 seconds
  8. Spin on Ice Cream, then re-spin once (add 15–30g milk if powdery)

Nutrition per Pint

175 Calories
15g Protein
22g Carbs
2g Fat

Pro Tips

  • “Sugar-free” pudding mixes vary by flavour and brand — adjust sweetener after tasting the liquid mix
  • If it spins powdery, add a splash of milk and re-spin (normal for very low-fat bases)
  • Great “base tech” for making instant flavours: chocolate, banana, butterscotch, etc.
