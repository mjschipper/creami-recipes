Lemon Bar Ice Cream

Bright, tangy lemon with sweet crust pieces

Calories 260
Protein 42g
Carbs 24g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 420 ml light high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 10 ml lemon juice
  • 5 g lemon zest
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 16 g vanilla whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 10 g skim milk powder
  • 40 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.8 g xanthan gum
  • 30 g lemon curd (see recipe)
  • 20 g crushed Granita biscuits

Instructions

  1. Make lemon curd: combine 60g lemon juice, zest of 1 lemon, 1 egg yolk, 20g erythritol, heat while whisking, add 1g xanthan gum, 5g butter, cool and freeze
  2. Make base with lemon juice and zest
  3. Freeze 24 hours
  4. Hot water rinse, spin on light ice cream
  5. Make hole in center
  6. Add frozen biscuit crumbs to bottom
  7. Drizzle frozen lemon curd in ribbons throughout
  8. Don't fully mix - want swirls
  9. Run mix-in setting briefly
  10. Top with extra biscuit crumbs

Nutrition per Pint

260 Calories
42g Protein
24g Carbs
5g Fat

Pro Tips

  • The lemon curd provides intense flavour + creamy texture
  • Fresh lemon juice and zest are essential - no bottled juice
