Ultimate Lemon Cheesecake Ice Cream

TRIPLE lemon punch: True Lemon + lemon pudding + cream cheese tang - professional quality!

Calories 320
Protein 44g
Carbs 30g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

Base

  • 340 ml 1% high protein milk
  • 60 g reduced-fat cream cheese (Philadelphia Light), softened
  • 2 packets True Lemon
  • 2 tbsp lemon instant pudding mix (sugar-free, Jell-O brand)
  • 5 ml (1 tsp) vanilla extract
  • 20 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g (⅛ tsp) salt

Graham Cracker Crust

  • 20 g Arnott's Granita biscuits, crushed and frozen

Instructions

  1. Soften cream cheese in microwave for 10-15 seconds (soft but not melted)
  2. Add milk to high-sided container
  3. Add softened cream cheese and blend with immersion blender for full 60 seconds until completely smooth (absolutely no lumps!)
  4. Add True Lemon packets and vanilla extract
  5. Blend 30 seconds to fully dissolve True Lemon crystals
  6. Add lemon pudding mix, erythritol, and salt
  7. Blend 90 seconds while dry ingredients incorporate
  8. Continue blending 90 more seconds - total 240 seconds blending time
  9. Taste - should be VERY tangy and lemony
  10. Let sit 5-10 minutes for foam to settle
  11. Pour into pint, cap, freeze 24 hours
  12. Hot water rinse 60 seconds before processing
  13. Process on Light Ice Cream setting
  14. Make hole on side of pint
  15. Add frozen crushed Granita biscuits
  16. Run Mix-In setting
  17. Top with extra graham cracker crumbs

Pro Tips

  • TRIPLE lemon system: True Lemon (brightness) + lemon pudding (body) + cream cheese (tang) = restaurant-quality lemon cheesecake
  • Cream cheese must be smooth: Full 60 seconds blending or you'll get lumps
  • Most intense lemon recipe: This is VERY lemony - if you prefer subtle, reduce True Lemon to 1 packet
  • Skip xanthan gum: Pudding mix + cream cheese provide all structure needed
  • Ultra-creamy texture: Combination creates silky, dense texture like real cheesecake
