Snickers Ice Cream

Peanuts, caramel, nougat, and chocolate - candy bar perfection

Calories 380
Protein 48g
Carbs 32g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 380 ml reduced fat high protein milk (A2 Protein Plus or Devondale Protein Plus)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 20 g natural peanut butter (smooth, unsweetened - 100% peanuts only)
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 2 ml peanut extract (optional but enhances peanut flavor)
  • 22 g vanilla whey protein
  • 30 g erythritol or Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.8 g xanthan gum
  • 30 g sugar-free caramel sauce (freeze until semi-solid)
  • 25 g roasted peanuts, roughly chopped and frozen
  • 15 g dark chocolate, melted with 5g coconut oil (for drizzle)

Instructions

  1. Prepare mix-ins first (Day 1 morning): Chop peanuts roughly (not too fine - want chunks), toast at 160°C (325°F) for 5 minutes if using raw, cool completely, freeze solid. Make sugar-free caramel sauce (see Salted Caramel recipe), freeze 30g portion until semi-solid (2 hours). Melt chocolate with coconut oil, set aside to cool
  2. Add milk, egg yolk, peanut butter, vanilla, peanut extract to container
  3. Blend with immersion blender for 90 seconds (peanut butter must fully emulsify)
  4. Mix dry ingredients in separate bowl
  5. Continue blending while slowly adding dry ingredients
  6. Mix additional 90 seconds after all ingredients added
  7. Let sit 10 minutes for foam to settle
  8. Pour into pint, cap and freeze 24 hours
  9. Run outside of pint under hot water for 60 seconds before spinning
  10. Spin on Light Ice Cream setting
  11. Work FAST from here (ice cream melts quickly): Make 3-4 holes throughout pint
  12. Place semi-solid caramel in ribbons (don't fully mix - want visible swirls)
  13. Add frozen peanut chunks throughout
  14. Drizzle melted chocolate in ribbons (it will freeze on contact)
  15. Run Mix-in setting briefly (5-7 seconds) - just to distribute
  16. Should see: caramel ribbons, chocolate swirls, peanut chunks

380 Calories
48g Protein
32g Carbs
12g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Peanut butter must be natural (just peanuts + salt) or it will separate - use Mayver's, Pic's - NOT Skippy/Jif
  • Warm peanut butter slightly (10 sec microwave) for easier blending
  • Work VERY fast during assembly - multiple components means more melt time
  • Don't fully mix caramel and chocolate - want distinct ribbons
  • Peanuts should be in chunks, not powder - freeze solid before adding
  • Adelaide Shopping: Peanuts (unsalted roasted or raw) at Coles/Woolworths. Peanut butter (100% peanuts only - Mayver's, Pic's). Peanut extract (Queen brand) at Coles/Woolworths (optional but enhances flavor). Dark chocolate (Lindt, Haigh's - local Adelaide!, or Cadbury)
  • Variations: Classic Snickers (use milk chocolate instead of dark - adds ~20 cal), Extra Peanutty (increase peanuts to 35g - adds ~55 cal), Snickers Extreme (add 15g chocolate chips as well - adds ~75 cal), Budget Version (skip peanut extract, use PB2 powder 15g instead of peanut butter - saves ~40 cal)
  • Difficulty: Medium-High (multiple components, timing matters)
  • Comparison to Real Snickers: Real Snickers ice cream pint: ~900-1,200 calories. Your version: 380 calories. Savings: 520-820 calories!
