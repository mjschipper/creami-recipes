Coffee Lovers Ice Cream

Rich espresso flavour without the café calories

Calories 240
Protein 46g
Carbs 16g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 420 ml 1% high protein milk
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 15 g instant espresso powder or 30g cold brew
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 22 g vanilla whey protein (2/3 scoop)
  • 35 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.8 g xanthan gum

Instructions

  1. Dissolve instant espresso in milk (or use cold brew)
  2. Add egg yolk and vanilla
  3. Blend with immersion blender
  4. Mix dry ingredients
  5. Blend while adding dry ingredients
  6. Mix 90 seconds
  7. Freeze 24 hours
  8. Process on light ice cream setting

Nutrition per Pint

240 Calories
46g Protein
16g Carbs
4g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Use high-quality instant espresso like Moccona Freeze Dried
  • Variations: Mocha (add cocoa), Tiramisu (add ladyfingers), Coffee Chip
