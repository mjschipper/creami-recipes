Simple Jaffa (True Citrus)

Perfect Australian classic - chocolate-orange Jaffa flavor using True Orange packets

Calories 240
Protein 30g
Carbs 22g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 420 ml high protein milk (or 400ml regular milk + 20g skim milk powder)
  • 2 packets True Orange (True Citrus Orange packets)
  • 32 g chocolate protein powder
  • 10 g cocoa powder (Cadbury Bournville recommended)
  • 40 g erythritol or Natvia
  • 1 g xanthan gum
  • 1 g salt
  • 5 ml vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Add milk and vanilla to high-sided container
  2. Dissolve True Orange packets in the milk (stir well to ensure crystals fully dissolve)
  3. Mix all dry ingredients in separate bowl
  4. Blend milk mixture with immersion blender
  5. Slowly add dry ingredients while blending
  6. Continue mixing 90 seconds after everything's incorporated
  7. Let sit 10 minutes for foam to settle
  8. Pour into pint, cap and freeze 24 hours minimum
  9. Run outside of pint under hot water for 60 seconds before spinning
  10. Spin on Sorbet setting (thickest consistency)

Nutrition per Pint

240 Calories
30g Protein
22g Carbs
5g Fat

Pro Tips

  • True Orange packets: Available at iHerb.com.au (best selection), Amazon.com.au, some Chemist Warehouse locations. Provides natural orange flavor without the need for fresh zest or extract
  • More Orange: Use 3 packets True Orange for stronger flavor
  • More Chocolate: Add 6 drops coffee extract + extra 2g cocoa powder
  • Sweeter: Add 10g real sugar (helps with aftertaste)
  • True Citrus Advantage: True Orange packets provide consistent, natural orange flavor without needing fresh oranges. No zesting required!
  • The orange flavor will be subtle when frozen - make the base taste slightly stronger than you want (frozen foods taste 30% less intense!)
  • Comparison to Original: This version uses True Orange packets instead of orange extract and zest, providing a more consistent and convenient orange flavor
