Lemon Cheesecake Swirl Ice Cream

Cheesecake-forward with True Lemon brightness - best of both worlds!

Calories 290
Protein 40g
Carbs 28g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

Cheesecake Base

  • 360 ml 1% high protein milk
  • 40 g reduced-fat cream cheese (Philadelphia Light), softened
  • 2 tbsp cheesecake instant pudding mix (sugar-free, Jell-O brand)
  • 2 packets True Lemon (or True Citrus Lemon)
  • 5 ml (1 tsp) vanilla extract
  • 20 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g (⅛ tsp) salt

Graham Cracker Crust

  • 20 g Arnott's Granita biscuits, crushed and frozen

Instructions

  1. Soften cream cheese in microwave for 10-15 seconds (should be soft but not melted)
  2. Add milk to high-sided container
  3. Add softened cream cheese and blend with immersion blender for full 60 seconds until completely smooth (no lumps!)
  4. Add True Lemon packets and vanilla extract
  5. Blend 30 seconds to fully dissolve True Lemon crystals
  6. Add cheesecake pudding mix, erythritol, and salt
  7. Blend 90 seconds while dry ingredients incorporate
  8. Continue blending 90 more seconds - total 240 seconds blending time
  9. Taste - should be tangy cheesecake with bright lemon notes
  10. Let sit 5-10 minutes for foam to settle
  11. Pour into pint, cap, freeze 24 hours
  12. Hot water rinse 60 seconds before processing
  13. Process on Light Ice Cream setting
  14. Make hole on side of pint
  15. Add frozen crushed Granita biscuits
  16. Run Mix-In setting
  17. Top with extra graham cracker crumbs if desired

Adelaide Shopping Guide

  • Cheesecake pudding mix: Jell-O brand at iHerb.com.au, USA Foods stores, Amazon.com.au (Aeroplane doesn't make this flavor)
  • True Lemon packets: iHerb.com.au (best selection), Amazon.com.au, some Chemist Warehouse
  • Cream cheese: Philadelphia Light (Coles, Woolworths - refrigerated section)
  • Granita biscuits: Arnott's brand (Coles, Woolworths biscuit aisle)
  • High protein milk: Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, Paul's Smarter White
  • Cost: ~$3.00-3.50 per pint

Pro Tips

  • Cheesecake pudding is KEY: Creates authentic NY cheesecake flavor - much creamier than just using cream cheese
  • True Lemon adds brightness: Cuts through richness of cheesecake without overwhelming it
  • Less cream cheese needed: Pudding mix provides cheesecake flavor, so only 40g cream cheese required vs 60g in other recipes
  • Ultra-creamy texture: Cheesecake pudding + cream cheese creates silky, dense texture
  • Adjust lemon intensity: Start with 1 True Lemon packet for subtle, use 2 for brighter flavor
  • Skip xanthan gum: Pudding mix + cream cheese provide all structure needed
  • Perfect balance: This is cheesecake that happens to have lemon, not lemon that happens to have cheesecake
