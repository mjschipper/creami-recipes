Simple Jaffa Chocolate Ice Cream

Perfect Australian classic - chocolate-orange Jaffa flavor

Calories 240
Protein 30g
Carbs 22g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 420 ml high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White) or 400ml regular full cream milk + 20g skim milk powder
  • 32 g chocolate protein powder
  • 10 g cocoa powder (Cadbury Bournville recommended)
  • 40 g erythritol or Natvia
  • 8 ml orange extract (Queen brand)
  • 2 g orange zest (fresh)
  • 1 g xanthan gum
  • 1 g salt
  • 5 ml vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Zest orange directly into high-sided container with milk
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in separate bowl
  3. Blend milk/zest with immersion blender
  4. Add orange extract and vanilla extract
  5. Slowly add dry ingredients while blending
  6. Continue mixing 90 seconds after everything's incorporated
  7. Let sit 10 minutes for foam to settle
  8. Pour into pint, cap and freeze 24 hours minimum
  9. Run outside of pint under hot water for 60 seconds before spinning
  10. Spin on Sorbet setting (thickest consistency)

Nutrition per Pint

240 Calories
30g Protein
22g Carbs
5g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Orange Extract: Queen brand available at Coles/Woolworths (baking aisle). The Essential Ingredient (78 Hutt St, Adelaide) has natural orange oils
  • More Orange: Increase extract to 10ml OR add 5g True Orange powder
  • More Chocolate: Add 6 drops coffee extract + extra 2g cocoa powder
  • Sweeter: Add 10g real sugar (helps with aftertaste from orange)
  • Fresh Zest: Zest directly into the milk for maximum flavor extraction
  • The orange flavor will be subtle when frozen - make the base taste slightly stronger than you want (frozen foods taste 30% less intense!)
