Butterscotch Butter Pecan Ice Cream

Rich butterscotch base with toasted pecans - classic indulgence

Calories 310
Protein 34g
Carbs 25g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

Base

  • 440 ml light high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 3 tbsp butterscotch instant pudding mix (sugar-free)
  • 5 ml (1 tsp) maple extract
  • 20 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g (⅛ tsp) salt

Pecan Mix-In

  • 30 g pecans, chopped and toasted

Instructions

  1. Toast pecans: Preheat oven to 150°C (300°F). Spread chopped pecans on baking sheet in single layer. Bake 8-10 minutes until fragrant and lightly golden (watch carefully - they burn quickly!)
  2. Remove from oven, cool completely on wire rack
  3. Once cool, freeze pecans solid (minimum 2 hours)
  4. Add milk and maple extract to high-sided container
  5. Add butterscotch pudding mix, erythritol, and salt
  6. Blend with immersion blender for 90 seconds (pudding needs time to hydrate)
  7. Continue blending additional 90 seconds - total 180 seconds
  8. Let sit 5-10 minutes for foam to settle
  9. Pour into pint, cap, freeze 24 hours
  10. Hot water rinse 60 seconds before processing
  11. Process on Light Ice Cream setting
  12. Make hole on side of pint
  13. Add frozen toasted pecans (work quickly!)
  14. Run Mix-In setting
  15. Re-spin if needed for desired consistency

Adelaide Shopping Guide

  • Butterscotch pudding mix: Jell-O brand at iHerb.com.au, USA Foods, Amazon.com.au (Aeroplane brand limited availability)
  • Pecans: Coles, Woolworths (nut aisle), Central Market (better bulk prices)
  • Maple extract: Queen brand (baking aisle) or vanilla extract works too
  • High protein milk: Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White (Coles, Woolworths), or Paul's Smarter White (Coles, Woolworths), or Paul's Smarter White (Coles, Woolworths), or Paul's Smarter White (Coles, Woolworths), or Paul's Smarter White (Coles, Woolworths), or Paul's Smarter White (Coles, Woolworths)
  • Cost: ~$3.50 per pint (pecans are expensive but worth it!)

Pro Tips

  • Toasting is essential: Raw pecans taste dull; toasted pecans are nutty, rich, aromatic
  • Watch the oven: Pecans go from perfect to burnt in 60 seconds - set a timer!
  • Freeze pecans solid: Room temp nuts get pulverized; frozen nuts stay chunky
  • Maple extract optional: Enhances "butter pecan" flavor but vanilla extract works too
  • Australian alternative: Use macadamias instead of pecans for Aussie twist (adjust cost up ~$2)
  • Skip xanthan gum: Pudding mix already contains stabilizers
