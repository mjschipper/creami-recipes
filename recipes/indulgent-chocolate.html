Indulgent Chocolate

Like Häagen-Dazs but with protein

Calories 480
Protein 32g
Carbs 38g
Freeze Time 36 hours

Ingredients

  • 280 ml reduced fat high protein milk (A2 Protein Plus or Devondale Protein Plus)
  • 120 ml heavy cream
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 6 drops coffee extract
  • 22 g chocolate whey protein (2/3 scoop)
  • 40 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 30 g real sugar
  • 15 g cocoa powder (3.5 tbsp)
  • 28 g semi-sweet chocolate, melted
  • 1 g salt
  • 0.8 g xanthan gum

Instructions

  1. Warm milk and cream to 27-32°C (80-90°F)
  2. Add egg yolks, vanilla, coffee extract
  3. Blend in melted chocolate
  4. Mix dry ingredients thoroughly
  5. Blend while adding dry ingredients
  6. Mix 90 seconds
  7. Freeze 36 hours (heated base)
  8. Process on light ice cream setting

Nutrition per Pint

480 Calories
32g Protein
38g Carbs
22g Fat
