True Citrus Lime Ice Cream

Sharp lime flavour using True Lime (or similar crystallised citrus)

Calories 160
Protein 15g
Carbs 18g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 240 ml 1% high protein milk
  • 200 ml unsweetened almond milk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 45 g erythritol or Natvia
  • 1 packet True Lime (or equivalent crystallised lime)
  • 1 g xanthan gum
  • 1 g salt

Instructions

  1. Add milk, almond milk and vanilla to a high-sided container
  2. Mix dry ingredients (erythritol, True Lime, xanthan gum, salt) in a separate bowl
  3. Blend wet ingredients, then slowly add dry mix while blending
  4. Continue blending for 90 seconds after everything is added
  5. Cap pint and freeze for 24 hours minimum
  6. Run outside of pint under hot water for 60 seconds
  7. Spin on Ice Cream, then re-spin to desired consistency

Nutrition per Pint

160 Calories
15g Protein
18g Carbs
2g Fat

Pro Tips

  • For “key lime pie” energy: add 5–10g lime juice and a pinch more salt, then mix in crushed biscuit
  • If it’s too sharp, increase sweetener slightly (cold mutes sweetness)
  • Optional: 1–2 drops green food colouring for the classic look
