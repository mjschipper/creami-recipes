Oreo McFlurry

McDonald's-inspired Oreo ice cream at home

Calories 280
Protein 52g
Carbs 26g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 430 ml 1% high protein milk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 32 g vanilla whey protein (1 scoop)
  • 20 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.5 g xanthan gum
  • 28 g mini Oreos (for mixing in)
  • 11 g mini Oreos (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Mix base using immersion blender method
  2. Freeze 24 hours
  3. Hot water rinse
  4. Spin on LIGHT ICE CREAM setting for soft-serve consistency
  5. Make 2.5cm (1 inch) hole in center
  6. Fill with 28g mini Oreos
  7. Cover with ice cream
  8. Run mix-in setting
  9. Chop remaining 11g Oreos
  10. Top pint with half the chopped Oreos
  11. Eat to 1/3 remaining, add rest of Oreos for chunks in every bite

Nutrition per Pint

280 Calories
52g Protein
26g Carbs
5g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Mini Oreos have thicker cookies which survive mixing better
