Violet Crumble (Honeycomb) Ice Cream

Adelaide-made icon by Robern Menz since 1913

Calories 340
Protein 46g
Carbs 32g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 410 ml light high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 16 g chocolate whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 8 g cocoa powder
  • 25 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.7 g xanthan gum
  • 1 Violet Crumble bar (50g), frozen and chopped

Instructions

  1. Add milk, egg yolk, and vanilla to high-sided container
  2. Mix protein, cocoa, erythritol, salt, xanthan gum in bowl
  3. Blend wet ingredients with immersion blender
  4. Slowly add dry ingredients while blending
  5. Mix additional 90 seconds
  6. Let sit 10 minutes, pour into pint, freeze 24 hours
  7. Freeze Violet Crumble bar until rock solid
  8. Chop into small chunks (work quickly - it shatters!)
  9. Hot water rinse 60 seconds
  10. Spin on light ice cream setting
  11. Make hole in center
  12. Add frozen Violet Crumble pieces
  13. Cover with ice cream, run mix-in setting
  14. Top with extra honeycomb pieces

Nutrition per Pint

340 Calories
46g Protein
32g Carbs
8g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Work quickly when chopping - honeycomb is temperature sensitive
  • This is an Adelaide icon - Robern Menz in Unley makes Violet Crumble!
