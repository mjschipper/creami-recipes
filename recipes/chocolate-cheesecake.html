Chocolate Cheesecake Ice Cream

Decadent chocolate-cream cheese fusion

Calories 340
Protein 46g
Carbs 28g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 360 ml light high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 60 ml fat-free cream cheese, softened
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 22 g chocolate whey protein (2/3 scoop)
  • 10 g skim milk powder
  • 12 g cocoa powder (3 tbsp)
  • 35 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.7 g xanthan gum
  • 6 drops coffee extract (optional)
  • 30 g graham cracker chunks

Instructions

  1. Blend milk and softened cream cheese until smooth
  2. Add egg yolk and vanilla, blend
  3. Mix all dry ingredients thoroughly
  4. Blend wet while slowly adding dry
  5. Mix additional 90 seconds
  6. Freeze 24 hours
  7. Process with graham crackers as standard

Nutrition per Pint

340 Calories
46g Protein
28g Carbs
8g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Alternative: Use chocolate graham crackers for the crust
