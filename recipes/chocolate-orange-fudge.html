Chocolate Orange Fudge Ice Cream

Terry's Chocolate Orange meets fudge brownie - dense, rich, incredible!

Calories 295
Protein 32g
Carbs 28g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

Fudge Base

  • 440 ml 1% high protein milk
  • 1 packet True Orange
  • 2 tbsp chocolate instant pudding mix (sugar-free, Jell-O or Aeroplane)
  • 10 g (2½ tbsp) cocoa powder (Cadbury Bournville or Callebaut)
  • 5 ml (1 tsp) vanilla extract
  • 25 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 1 g (¼ tsp) salt

Chocolate Chip Mix-In

  • 20 g dark chocolate chips (Lily's sugar-free or Lindt 85%), frozen solid

Instructions

  1. Add milk, True Orange packet, and vanilla to high-sided container
  2. Blend with immersion blender for 30 seconds to fully dissolve True Orange crystals
  3. Mix chocolate pudding, cocoa powder, erythritol, and salt in separate bowl
  4. Slowly add dry ingredients while blending (cocoa clumps if added too fast)
  5. Blend 90 seconds while dry ingredients incorporate
  6. Continue blending 90 more seconds - total 180 seconds blending time
  7. Taste - should be rich chocolate with subtle orange notes
  8. Let sit 5-10 minutes for foam to settle
  9. Pour into pint, cap, freeze 24 hours
  10. Hot water rinse 60 seconds before processing
  11. Process on SORBET setting (creates densest, fudgiest texture - key to this recipe!)
  12. Make hole in center of pint
  13. Quickly add frozen dark chocolate chips (work fast - base melts quickly after spinning)
  14. Run Mix-In setting
  15. Top with extra chocolate shavings if desired

  • Chocolate pudding: Jell-O (iHerb, USA Foods) or Aeroplane (Coles, Woolworths)
  • Premium cocoa: Cadbury Bournville (Coles, Woolworths) or Callebaut (The Essential Ingredient, 78 Hutt St)
  • Dark chocolate chips: Lily's (health food stores), Lindt 85% chopped (Coles, Woolworths)

  • High protein milk: Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus
  • Cost: ~$3.00 per pint

Pro Tips

  • SORBET setting is critical: Creates fudge brownie-like density - don't use Light Ice Cream!
  • True Orange + chocolate combo: Orange enhances chocolate depth without tasting "orange" - just makes chocolate richer
  • Pudding mix creates fudge: Combined with Sorbet setting, creates dense, fudgy texture
  • Premium cocoa essential: Don't cheap out - Cadbury Bournville or better makes huge difference
  • Skip xanthan gum: Pudding mix provides all stabilization needed
  • Ben & Jerry's texture: Dense, fudgy, scoopable - not icy at all
