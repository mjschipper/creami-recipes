Tim Tam Slam Ice Cream

Australia's favourite biscuit in frozen form

Calories 320
Protein 48g
Carbs 28g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 410g 2% high protein milk
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5g vanilla extract (1 tsp)
  • 16g chocolate whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 10g cocoa powder (2 tbsp)
  • 25g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5g salt (1/8 tsp)
  • 0.7g xanthan gum
  • 2 Tim Tam Original biscuits (40g), frozen solid

Instructions

  1. Add milk, egg yolk, and vanilla to high-sided container
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in separate bowl
  3. Blend wet ingredients with immersion blender
  4. Slowly add dry ingredients while blending
  5. Mix additional 90 seconds
  6. Let sit 10 minutes for foam to settle
  7. Pour into pint, freeze 24 hours
  8. Hot water rinse 60 seconds
  9. Spin on light ice cream setting
  10. Make hole near side of pint (preserve chunks)
  11. Break frozen Tim Tams into 6-8 pieces each
  12. Add to hole, push toward bottom
  13. Cover with ice cream, run mix-in setting
  14. Top with extra Tim Tam crumbs if desired

Nutrition per Pint

320 Calories
48g Protein
28g Carbs
8g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Freeze Tim Tams solid for 24+ hours alongside the pint
  • Try different varieties - Dark, Caramel, or Double Coat work great!
