Strawberry Cheesecake Ice Cream

Summer classic with fresh berry swirls

Calories 330
Protein 44g
Carbs 30g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 380 ml 1% high protein milk
  • 60 ml fat-free cream cheese, softened
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 2.5 ml lemon juice
  • 16 g vanilla whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 10 ml fat-free milk powder
  • 35 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.7 g xanthan gum
  • 22 g graham cracker chunks
  • 30 g strawberry sauce

Instructions

  1. Make strawberry sauce: blend 150g strawberries, 20g erythritol, 5g lemon juice, heat and add 2g xanthan gum, cool and freeze
  2. Make cheesecake base as per New York Cheesecake
  3. Freeze 24 hours
  4. Hot water rinse, spin on light ice cream
  5. Make hole in center, remove some ice cream
  6. Add 22g frozen graham crackers to bottom
  7. Drizzle 30g frozen strawberry sauce in ribbons
  8. Add ice cream back, don't fully mix - want swirls
  9. Run mix-in setting briefly (5-7 seconds less)
  10. Top with extra crackers and drizzle of sauce

Nutrition per Pint

330 Calories
44g Protein
30g Carbs
6g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Don't over-mix - the swirls are the signature
