Indulgent Vanilla Bean

Rivals premium gelato - for special occasions

Calories 420
Protein 30g
Carbs 32g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 300 ml 2% high protein milk
  • 100 ml heavy cream
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 whole vanilla bean (seeds scraped)
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 16 g vanilla whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 30 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 20 g real sugar (adds smoothness)
  • 1 g xanthan gum
  • 1 g salt

Instructions

  1. Follow Vanilla Bean Base method
  2. Heavy cream adds serious richness
  3. Real sugar smooths texture and reduces ice crystals
  4. Freeze 24 hours, process as normal

Nutrition per Pint

420 Calories
30g Protein
32g Carbs
18g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Low-Cal Vanilla Bean: 200 cal, 28g protein
  • Indulgent Vanilla Bean: 420 cal, 30g protein
  • Difference: +220 calories for restaurant-quality creaminess
