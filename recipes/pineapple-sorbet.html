Pineapple Sorbet (Dole Whip Style)

Disney-inspired refreshment - sweet and tangy

Calories 120
Protein 1g
Carbs 30g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 300g fresh pineapple chunks (or canned in juice)
  • 140g pineapple juice (reserved or from can)
  • 20g erythritol/Natvia (or 15g agave)
  • 0.5g salt (1/8 tsp)
  • 5ml lime juice (optional)
  • 2.5ml vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions

  1. Twist crown off pineapple, cut off top and bottom
  2. Slice off skin following curve, remove eyes
  3. Quarter lengthwise, cut out tough core
  4. Chop into chunks, save all juice
  5. Add 300g pineapple chunks to high-sided container
  6. Add 140g pineapple juice, sweetener, salt, lime, vanilla
  7. Blend with immersion blender 90 seconds until silky smooth
  8. Taste - should be sweeter than desired
  9. Pour into pint, cap, freeze 24 hours
  10. Hot water rinse 60 seconds
  11. Spin on Sorbet, re-spin 2-3 times for ultra-smooth

Nutrition per Pint

120 Calories
1g Protein
30g Carbs
0g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Tastes like Disneyland's famous Dole Whip!
  • Budget option: Use quality canned pineapple in juice (not syrup)
