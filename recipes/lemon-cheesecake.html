Lemon Cheesecake Ice Cream

Bright, tangy, and refreshing

Calories 315
Protein 45g
Carbs 26g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 380 ml light high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 60 ml fat-free cream cheese, softened
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 10 ml fresh lemon juice
  • 5 g lemon zest
  • 16 g vanilla whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 10 g skim milk powder
  • 40 g erythritol/Natvia (increased for tartness)
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.7 g xanthan gum
  • 2 drops yellow food colouring (optional)
  • 30 g graham cracker chunks

Instructions

  1. Zest lemon directly into milk for maximum flavour
  2. Follow standard cheesecake base method
  3. Lemon juice and zest add authentic brightness
  4. Process with vanilla graham crackers

Nutrition per Pint

315 Calories
45g Protein
26g Carbs
6g Fat

Pro Tips

  • This tastes even better after 2-3 days as flavours meld
