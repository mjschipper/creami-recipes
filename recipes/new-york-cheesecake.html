New York Cheesecake Ice Cream

Rich, creamy, and tangy - just like the real thing

Calories 310
Protein 45g
Carbs 26g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 380 ml 1% high protein milk
  • 60 ml fat-free cream cheese, softened
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 2.5 ml lemon juice
  • 16 g vanilla whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 10 ml fat-free milk powder
  • 35 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.7 g xanthan gum
  • 30 g graham cracker crust chunks

Instructions

  1. Add milk and softened cream cheese to container
  2. Blend with immersion blender until completely smooth (60 seconds)
  3. Add egg yolk, vanilla extract, and lemon juice
  4. Blend 30 seconds
  5. Mix all dry ingredients in separate bowl
  6. Start blending wet ingredients, slowly add dry
  7. Mix additional 90 seconds
  8. Let sit 10 minutes for foam to settle
  9. Pour into pint and freeze 24 hours
  10. Hot water rinse 1 minute, spin on light ice cream
  11. Make 2.5cm hole in center
  12. Add 22g frozen crust chunks, push to bottom
  13. Cover with ice cream, run mix-in setting
  14. Top with extra 8g crust crumbs

Nutrition per Pint

310 Calories
45g Protein
26g Carbs
6g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Cream cheese MUST be softened - microwave 10-15 seconds if needed
  • Lemon juice is essential for authentic cheesecake tang
  • Use Arnott's Granita biscuits for crust in Australia
