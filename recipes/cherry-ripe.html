Cherry Ripe Ice Cream

Australia's oldest chocolate bar (1924) in ice cream form

Calories 330
Protein 40g
Carbs 34g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 380 ml light high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 16 g chocolate whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 10 g cocoa powder
  • 25 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.7 g xanthan gum
  • 30 g glacé cherries, halved and frozen
  • 20 g desiccated coconut
  • 15 g dark chocolate, chopped and frozen

Instructions

  1. Add milk, egg yolk, and vanilla to high-sided container
  2. Mix protein, cocoa, erythritol, salt, xanthan gum in bowl
  3. Blend wet ingredients with immersion blender
  4. Slowly add dry ingredients while blending
  5. Mix additional 90 seconds
  6. Let sit 10 minutes, pour into pint, freeze 24 hours
  7. Prepare mix-ins: halve glacé cherries, chop chocolate, freeze all components
  8. Hot water rinse 60 seconds
  9. Spin on light ice cream setting
  10. Make hole in center
  11. Layer: coconut, cherries, chocolate, repeat
  12. Cover with ice cream, run mix-in setting
  13. Top with extra cherry halves

Nutrition per Pint

330 Calories
40g Protein
34g Carbs
10g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Glacé cherries work best - fresh cherries add too much moisture
  • The dark chocolate and coconut combination is essential
