Raspberry Sorbet

Elegant and tart - requires straining for seeds

Calories 105
Protein 2g
Carbs 22g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 230 g fresh raspberries
  • 210 ml water
  • 40 g erythritol/Natvia (more due to tartness)
  • 0.5 g salt

Instructions

  1. Rinse raspberries gently
  2. Add raspberries, water, erythritol, and salt to high-sided container
  3. Use immersion blender to completely crush (90 seconds)
  4. Place strainer over pint
  5. Pour mixture through strainer into pint
  6. Option: Add half seeds back to middle for texture
  7. Cap and freeze 24 hours
  8. Hot water rinse 60 seconds
  9. Spin on Sorbet, re-spin twice

Nutrition per Pint

105 Calories
2g Protein
22g Carbs
0g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Straining removes seeds that can make it chewy
  • Adding half seeds back provides texture + fibre
