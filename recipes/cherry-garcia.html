Cherry Garcia

Ben & Jerry's copycat with half the calories

Calories 450
Protein 45g
Carbs 42g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 20 ml bing cherry juice (from jar)
  • 380 ml reduced fat high protein milk (A2 Protein Plus or Devondale Protein Plus)
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 16 g vanilla whey-casein blend (1/2 scoop)
  • 40 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.8 g xanthan gum
  • 5 bing cherries, prepared
  • 15 g dark chocolate, chopped

Instructions

  1. Add cherry juice, milk, egg yolks, vanilla to container
  2. Mix dry ingredients in bowl
  3. Blend wet with immersion blender while slowly adding dry
  4. Mix additional 90 seconds
  5. Pour into pint, freeze 24 hours
  6. Prepare cherries: remove stems, open each cherry, press into TRIPLE-layer paper towels to absorb juice
  7. Chop dark chocolate into 3mm chunks
  8. Hot water rinse 1 minute
  9. Spin on SORBET setting (thickest for Ben & Jerry's style)
  10. Make large hole in center
  11. Layer: chocolate, then cherries, repeat
  12. Cover with ice cream
  13. Run mix-in setting

Nutrition per Pint

450 Calories
45g Protein
42g Carbs
12g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Paper towel technique is CRITICAL - excess juice creates icy texture
  • Use 2 egg yolks (no whites) for creaminess without extra water
  • Sorbet setting creates Ben & Jerry's thick consistency
