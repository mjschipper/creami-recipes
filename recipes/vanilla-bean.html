Vanilla Bean Ice Cream

Restaurant-quality luxury with real vanilla bean seeds

Calories 200
Protein 28g
Carbs 18g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 430 ml reduced fat high protein milk (A2 Protein Plus or Devondale Protein Plus)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/2 vanilla bean, seeds scraped
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 45 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 1 g xanthan gum
  • 1 g salt

Instructions

  1. Add milk, egg yolk, and vanilla bean seeds to container
  2. Add vanilla extract
  3. Mix dry ingredients separately
  4. Use immersion blender - critical for vanilla bean distribution
  5. Blend wet ingredients while slowly adding dry
  6. Mix additional 90 seconds
  7. Let sit 10 minutes for foam to settle
  8. Pour into pint and freeze 24 hours
  9. Hot water rinse, spin on ice cream setting
  10. Mix-in or re-spin to desired consistency

Nutrition per Pint

200 Calories
28g Protein
18g Carbs
5g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Egg yolk is a natural emulsifier like in gelato - dramatically increases creaminess
  • The immersion blender is critical for proper vanilla bean distribution
  • Worth the extra 50 calories for the rich mouthfeel
