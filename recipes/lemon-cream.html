Lemon Cream Ice Cream

Bright, creamy lemon flavor that perfectly masks erythritol aftertaste

Calories 180
Protein 28g
Carbs 20g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 440 ml light high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 5 ml (1 tsp) vanilla extract
  • 1-2 packets True Lemon (start with 1, taste, add 2nd if needed)
  • 16 g (~½ scoop) vanilla whey protein powder
  • 10 g (2 tbsp) skim milk powder
  • 30 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 1 g (¼ tsp) salt
  • 0.7 g (scant ¼ tsp) xanthan gum

Instructions

  1. Add milk and vanilla to high-sided container
  2. Dissolve True Lemon packets in milk (stir well to fully dissolve crystals)
  3. Mix all dry ingredients in separate bowl
  4. Blend wet ingredients with immersion blender
  5. Slowly add dry ingredients while blending
  6. Mix additional 90 seconds after all ingredients added
  7. Taste - should be bright, lemony, and sweeter than desired
  8. Pour into pint, cap, freeze 24 hours
  9. Hot water rinse 60 seconds before spinning
  10. Process on Light Ice Cream setting
  11. Re-spin if needed for desired consistency

Pro Tips

  • Perfect for erythritol haters: Lemon brightness completely masks any cooling aftertaste
  • Start conservative: 1 packet is subtle, 2 packets is bright - taste before freezing to decide
  • Enhances vanilla base: Works as flavor booster even in other recipes
  • Refreshing summer flavor: Light, bright, perfect for Adelaide's hot weather
  • No liquid issues: True Lemon is crystallized so doesn't affect base ratios
  • Budget friendly: Each packet costs ~$0.20, minimal cost increase
