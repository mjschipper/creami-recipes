Oreo Cheesecake Ice Cream

Cookies and cream meets New York cheesecake

Calories 380
Protein 47g
Carbs 34g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 380g 1% high protein milk
  • 60g fat-free cream cheese, softened
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5g vanilla extract (1 tsp)
  • 2.5g lemon juice (1/2 tsp)
  • 16g vanilla whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 10g fat-free milk powder
  • 35g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5g salt (1/8 tsp)
  • 0.7g xanthan gum
  • 28g mini Oreos, frozen solid
  • 11g mini Oreos for topping

Instructions

  1. Make cheesecake base, freeze 24 hours
  2. Hot water rinse, spin on light ice cream
  3. Make hole off-center (preserve cookie chunks)
  4. Add frozen mini Oreos
  5. Cover with ice cream, run mix-in setting
  6. Top with crushed Oreos instead of graham crackers

Nutrition per Pint

380 Calories
47g Protein
34g Carbs
8g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Skip graham crackers - Oreos provide the crunch
