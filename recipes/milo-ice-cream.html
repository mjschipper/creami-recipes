Milo Ice Cream

Australian childhood classic in frozen form

Calories 250
Protein 46g
Carbs 24g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 420 ml 1% high protein milk
  • 25 g Milo powder
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 32 g vanilla or chocolate whey protein (1 scoop)
  • 15 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.7 g xanthan gum
  • 10 g extra Milo for topping

Instructions

  1. Add milk, Milo powder, and vanilla to high-sided container
  2. Mix protein powder, erythritol, salt, xanthan gum in separate bowl
  3. Blend wet ingredients with immersion blender
  4. Slowly add dry ingredients while blending
  5. Mix additional 90 seconds
  6. Let sit 10 minutes for foam to settle
  7. Pour into pint, freeze 24 hours
  8. Hot water rinse 60 seconds
  9. Spin on light ice cream setting
  10. Re-spin for desired consistency
  11. Top with extra Milo powder

Nutrition per Pint

250 Calories
46g Protein
24g Carbs
5g Fat

Pro Tips

  • The malty, chocolatey Milo flavour comes through perfectly
  • For extra crunch, sprinkle Milo on top just before eating
