Indulgent Peanut Butter Cup

Reese's lovers, this is your pint

Calories 620
Protein 54g
Carbs 36g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 300 ml reduced fat high protein milk (A2 Protein Plus or Devondale Protein Plus)
  • 60 ml heavy cream
  • 60 g natural peanut butter (4 tbsp)
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 22 g vanilla whey protein (2/3 scoop)
  • 35 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.8 g xanthan gum
  • 40 g chocolate chips
  • 30 g peanut butter cups, frozen and chopped

Instructions

  1. Make peanut butter base with cream
  2. Freeze 24 hours
  3. Spin on sorbet (thick base needs it)
  4. Layer chocolate chips and PB cup pieces
  5. Run mix-in setting
  6. Top with extra PB cup pieces

Nutrition per Pint

620 Calories
54g Protein
36g Carbs
32g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Ultimate indulgence for peanut butter lovers
  • Reserve for special occasions or maintenance phase
