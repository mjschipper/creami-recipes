Banana Cream Ice Cream

Concentrated banana cream flavor - tastes like banana cream pie!

Calories 270
Protein 32g
Carbs 35g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 380 ml light high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 60 g (~½ medium) very ripe banana, mashed (brown spots essential!)
  • 2 tbsp banana cream instant pudding mix (sugar-free, Jell-O brand)
  • 5 ml (1 tsp) vanilla extract
  • 15 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g (⅛ tsp) salt

Instructions

  1. Choose banana: Must have brown spots! Green or yellow bananas won't work - need natural sugars developed from ripening
  2. Mash banana thoroughly with fork until completely smooth (no chunks)
  3. Add milk, mashed banana, and vanilla to high-sided container
  4. Blend with immersion blender for 60 seconds until banana fully incorporated (banana must be completely blended or you'll get icy chunks)
  5. Add pudding mix, erythritol, and salt
  6. Blend additional 90 seconds while adding dry ingredients
  7. Continue blending 90 more seconds - total 240 seconds blending (banana + pudding need thorough hydration)
  8. Let sit 5-10 minutes for foam to settle
  9. Pour into pint, cap, freeze 24 hours
  10. Hot water rinse 60 seconds before processing
  11. Process on Light Ice Cream setting
  12. Re-spin if needed for desired consistency

Pro Tips

  • Ripeness is CRITICAL: Brown-spotted bananas are 40% sweeter than yellow bananas
  • Pudding + banana combo: Creates concentrated banana cream flavor - better than using just banana
  • Reduced banana amount: Using less banana (60g vs 80g) with pudding prevents icy texture
  • Blend thoroughly: Banana chunks freeze rock solid - must be completely smooth
  • Skip xanthan gum: Pudding mix contains stabilizers already
  • Tastes like pie: Flavor profile matches banana cream pie filling perfectly
