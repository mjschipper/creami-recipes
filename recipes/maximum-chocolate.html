Maximum Chocolate Ice Cream

Restaurant-quality with melted chocolate - deep, rich flavour

Calories 240
Protein 30g
Carbs 22g
Freeze Time 36 hours

Ingredients

  • 410 g 1% high protein milk
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2.1 g vanilla extract
  • 50 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 10 g cocoa powder (2.5 tbsp)
  • 1 g salt
  • 1 g xanthan gum
  • 14 g semi-sweet chocolate, melted

Instructions

  1. Combine milk, egg yolk, vanilla in container
  2. Mix dry ingredients in bowl
  3. Melt chocolate in microwave (45-60 seconds)
  4. Microwave milk mixture 30-60 seconds to 27-32°C (80-90°F)
  5. Pour melted chocolate into warm milk
  6. Blend 30 seconds with immersion blender
  7. Slowly add dry ingredients while blending
  8. Mix additional 90 seconds
  9. Optional: Strain to catch any chocolate chunks
  10. Wait 10 minutes before capping
  11. Freeze 36 HOURS (heated liquid needs longer)
  12. Hot water rinse, spin on light ice cream
  13. Mix-in for perfect consistency

Nutrition per Pint

240 Calories
30g Protein
22g Carbs
7g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Critical: 36-hour freeze time for heated recipes
  • Check temperature carefully - too hot will cook the egg
