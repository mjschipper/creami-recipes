Salted Caramel Ice Cream

Sweet and salty perfection with caramel swirls

Calories 300
Protein 46g
Carbs 22g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 420g 2% high protein milk
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 4.2g vanilla extract (1 tsp)
  • 16g vanilla whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 10g fat-free milk powder
  • 30g erythritol/Natvia (less - caramel adds sweetness)
  • 1g salt (1/4 tsp - important for salted caramel)
  • 0.8g xanthan gum
  • 30g sugar-free caramel sauce

Instructions

  1. Make vanilla base following standard method
  2. Freeze 24 hours
  3. Hot water rinse, spin on light ice cream
  4. Make small holes throughout pint
  5. Drizzle 30g frozen caramel sauce in ribbons
  6. Don't fully mix - want caramel swirls
  7. Run mix-in setting briefly to preserve swirls
  8. Drizzle extra caramel on top
  9. Sprinkle flaky sea salt over top

Nutrition per Pint

300 Calories
46g Protein
22g Carbs
6g Fat

Pro Tips

  • The salt is ESSENTIAL - don't skip or reduce
  • Flaky sea salt on top gives restaurant presentation
