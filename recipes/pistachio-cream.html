Pistachio Cream

Creamy, nutty, and absolutely addictive

Calories 290
Protein 46g
Carbs 20g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 400 ml reduced fat high protein milk (A2 Protein Plus or Devondale Protein Plus)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 30 g pistachio butter or 40g ground pistachios
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 2-3 drops almond extract
  • 16 g vanilla whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 8 g skim milk powder
  • 35 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.8 g xanthan gum
  • 2 drops green food colouring (optional)

Instructions

  1. If using whole pistachios: grind in food processor until very fine
  2. Add milk, egg yolk, pistachio butter, vanilla, almond extract to container
  3. Blend with immersion blender for 60 seconds
  4. Mix dry ingredients in bowl
  5. Continue blending while slowly adding dry ingredients
  6. Mix additional 90 seconds
  7. Strain through fine mesh to remove any chunks (optional)
  8. Let sit 10 minutes, pour into pint
  9. Freeze 24 hours
  10. Hot water rinse, spin on light ice cream
  11. Re-spin for desired consistency

Nutrition per Pint

290 Calories
46g Protein
20g Carbs
8g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Pistachio butter makes this MUCH easier
  • Almond extract is key - brings out pistachio flavour
  • Can add 15g chopped pistachios as mix-in for crunch
