Chocolate Orange Ice Cream

Terry's Chocolate Orange inspired - rich chocolate meets bright citrus

Calories 195
Protein 28g
Carbs 21g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 440 ml 1% high protein milk
  • 5 ml (1 tsp) vanilla extract
  • 1 packet True Orange
  • 50 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 10 g (2½ tbsp) cocoa powder (Cadbury Bournville or Callebaut)
  • 1 g (¼ tsp) salt
  • 1 g (¼ tsp) xanthan gum

Instructions

  1. Add milk and vanilla to high-sided container
  2. Dissolve True Orange packet in milk (stir well)
  3. Mix cocoa powder, erythritol, salt, and xanthan gum in separate bowl
  4. Blend wet ingredients with immersion blender
  5. Slowly add dry ingredients while blending (cocoa can clump if added too fast)
  6. Mix additional 90 seconds after all ingredients added
  7. Taste - should have balanced chocolate-orange flavor
  8. Pour into pint, cap, freeze 24 hours
  9. Hot water rinse 60 seconds before spinning
  10. Process on Light Ice Cream setting
  11. Re-spin if needed for desired consistency

Adelaide Shopping Guide

  • True Orange: iHerb.com.au, Amazon.com.au
  • Premium cocoa: Cadbury Bournville (Coles, Woolworths) or Callebaut (The Essential Ingredient, 78 Hutt St)
  • High protein milk: Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus
  • Natvia: Coles, Woolworths

Pro Tips

  • Orange enhances chocolate: Citrus notes make chocolate taste richer and more complex
  • Use quality cocoa: Cadbury Bournville or Callebaut make huge difference
  • Don't overdo orange: 1 packet is perfect - more makes it taste artificial
  • Terry's dupe: Tastes remarkably like the iconic chocolate orange candy
  • Adelaide twist: Try with Haigh's dark chocolate shavings as topping
  • Cost effective: ~$2.20 per pint vs $15+ for Terry's Chocolate Orange
