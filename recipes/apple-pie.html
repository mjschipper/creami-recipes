Apple Pie Ice Cream

Warm spices and apple chunks - dessert in a pint

Calories 280
Protein 44g
Carbs 30g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 420 ml light high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 3 g cinnamon
  • 1 g nutmeg
  • 16 g vanilla whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 10 g skim milk powder
  • 35 g erythritol brown sugar
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.8 g xanthan gum
  • 120 g prepared apple mix-in
  • 20 g crushed Granita biscuits (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare apples: peel and dice 1 small Granny Smith into 5mm cubes
  2. Mix apple with 10g sugar, 3g cinnamon, 1g nutmeg, pinch cloves, 5g lemon juice
  3. Microwave 2-3 minutes until soft but not mushy
  4. Cool completely, freeze on parchment until solid
  5. Make base with cinnamon and nutmeg in dry ingredients
  6. Freeze 24 hours
  7. Hot water rinse, spin on light ice cream
  8. Make hole off-center
  9. Add frozen apple chunks, push toward bottom
  10. Add crushed biscuits if using
  11. Cover with ice cream, run mix-in setting
  12. Top with extra cinnamon

Nutrition per Pint

280 Calories
44g Protein
30g Carbs
4g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Don't skip freezing the apples - warm apples make ice cream watery
  • Granny Smith apples best for tartness that balances sweetness
