Mocha Swirl Ice Cream

Coffee and chocolate ribbons - café-quality

Calories 270
Protein 48g
Carbs 22g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 420g 1% high protein milk
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 15g instant espresso powder
  • 4.2g vanilla extract (1 tsp)
  • 22g vanilla whey protein (2/3 scoop)
  • 35g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5g salt (1/8 tsp)
  • 0.8g xanthan gum
  • 40g chocolate sauce (see recipe)

Instructions

  1. Make chocolate sauce: melt 20g chocolate, whisk in 10g cocoa, 20g erythritol, 30g water, 5g butter, cool and freeze
  2. Dissolve espresso powder in milk
  3. Make coffee base following standard method
  4. Freeze 24 hours
  5. Hot water rinse, spin on light ice cream
  6. Make hole in center
  7. Drizzle frozen chocolate sauce in ribbons
  8. Don't fully mix - want distinct swirls
  9. Run mix-in setting briefly (5-7 seconds less)
  10. Top with chocolate shavings

Nutrition per Pint

270 Calories
48g Protein
22g Carbs
6g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Variations: White Chocolate Mocha, Caramel Mocha, Peppermint Mocha
