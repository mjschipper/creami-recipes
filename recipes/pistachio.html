Pistachio Ice Cream

Classic pistachio flavor using instant pudding mix - no artificial taste!

Calories 200
Protein 32g
Carbs 22g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

Base

  • 440 ml light high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 3 tbsp pistachio instant pudding mix (sugar-free, Jell-O brand recommended)
  • 5 ml (1 tsp) almond extract
  • 20 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g (⅛ tsp) salt

Optional Mix-In

  • 20 g pistachios, shelled, roughly chopped, and frozen

Note: Mix-in adds ~110 calories, 4g protein, 7g fat

Instructions

  1. Add milk and almond extract to high-sided container
  2. Add pudding mix, erythritol, and salt
  3. Blend with immersion blender for 90 seconds (pudding mix needs time to fully hydrate)
  4. Continue blending additional 90 seconds - total 180 seconds blending time
  5. Let sit 5-10 minutes for any foam to settle
  6. Pour into pint, cap, freeze 24 hours
  7. Hot water rinse 60 seconds before processing
  8. Process on Light Ice Cream setting
  9. Optional: Make hole on side, add frozen chopped pistachios, run Mix-In setting
  10. Re-spin if needed for desired consistency

Pro Tips

  • Pudding mix is the secret: Provides authentic pistachio flavor without artificial taste
  • Skip xanthan gum: Pudding mix already contains stabilizers - adding more creates gummy texture
  • Almond extract enhances: Complements pistachio flavor beautifully
  • Blend thoroughly: Full 180 seconds ensures pudding mix fully hydrates
  • Mix-in is optional: Base alone tastes great, but real pistachios add luxury
  • Natural green color: Pudding mix provides pale green color naturally
