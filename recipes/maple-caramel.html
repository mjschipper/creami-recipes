Maple Caramel Ice Cream

Canadian-Australian fusion - two swirls of heaven

Calories 320
Protein 46g
Carbs 28g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 400 ml reduced fat high protein milk (A2 Protein Plus or Devondale Protein Plus)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 15 ml sugar-free maple syrup (Lakanto, Skinny Syrups, or Queen extract)
  • 22 g vanilla whey protein
  • 25 g erythritol or Natvia (reduced because maple syrup adds sweetness)
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.8 g xanthan gum
  • 30 g sugar-free caramel sauce (freeze until semi-solid)
  • 15 g walnuts, roughly chopped, toasted and frozen (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add milk, egg yolk, vanilla, maple syrup to container
  2. Blend with immersion blender
  3. Mix dry ingredients separately
  4. Continue blending while adding dry ingredients
  5. Mix 90 seconds, TASTE TEST: Should taste noticeably maple (add 5ml more if too subtle)
  6. Let sit 10 minutes for foam to settle
  7. Pour into pint, cap and freeze 24 hours
  8. Prepare caramel: Make sugar-free caramel sauce (see Salted Caramel recipe), freeze 30g portion until semi-solid (2 hours)
  9. Run outside of pint under hot water for 60 seconds before spinning
  10. Spin on Light Ice Cream setting
  11. Make 2-3 holes throughout pint, use spoon to place semi-solid caramel in ribbons
  12. Don't mix fully - want distinct caramel swirls
  13. Add walnut pieces if using, run Mix-in setting briefly (5 seconds)
  14. Should see amber caramel ribbons with maple-flavored base

Nutrition per Pint

320 Calories
46g Protein
28g Carbs
7g Fat

Pro Tips

  • If using maple extract instead of syrup, start with 5ml (1 tsp) - adds NO calories
  • Caramel swirl should be visible, not fully mixed
  • Walnuts are traditional but optional - still delicious without (with walnuts: ~420 calories, 48g protein)
  • This tastes like pancakes and caramel had a baby
  • Adelaide Shopping: Sugar-free maple syrup (Lakanto at Chemist Warehouse, or Skinny Syrups online). Alternative: Queen Maple Extract (5ml instead of syrup). Caramel sauce: Make your own or Walden Farms (online)
  • Variations: Maple Pecan (use pecans instead of walnuts), Maple Bacon (add 20g cooked, crumbled bacon - adds ~100 cal but AMAZING), Maple Cinnamon (add 3g cinnamon to base)
