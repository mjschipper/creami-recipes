Almond Joy Ice Cream

Chocolate, coconut, and almonds - candy bar nostalgia

Calories 340
Protein 46g
Carbs 26g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 400 ml light high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 4-5 drops coconut extract (optional)
  • 22 g chocolate whey protein (2/3 scoop)
  • 40 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 10 g cocoa powder
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.8 g xanthan gum
  • 30 g toasted coconut mixture
  • 20 g almonds, chopped and frozen
  • 15 g dark chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare coconut: mix 30g shredded coconut, 15g melted coconut oil, 10g erythritol, 2g vanilla
  2. Toast in 150°C (300°F) oven 8-10 minutes, cool, freeze
  3. Make chocolate base
  4. Freeze 24 hours
  5. Hot water rinse, spin on light ice cream
  6. Make hole in center
  7. Layer: coconut, almonds, chocolate chips, coconut, almonds
  8. Cover with ice cream, run mix-in setting

Nutrition per Pint

340 Calories
46g Protein
26g Carbs
12g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Australian variation: Use macadamias instead of almonds
  • Bounty Bar style: Increase coconut to 40g, skip almonds
