Key Lime Pie Ice Cream

Restaurant-quality key lime pie with graham cracker crust - tangy, creamy perfection!

Calories 310
Protein 40g
Carbs 28g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

Base

  • 360 ml 1% high protein milk
  • 60 g reduced-fat cream cheese, softened
  • 5 ml (1 tsp) vanilla extract
  • 2 packets True Lime (or True Citrus Lime)
  • 2 tbsp vanilla instant pudding mix (sugar-free, Aeroplane or Jell-O)
  • 16 g (~½ scoop) vanilla whey protein powder
  • 25 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g (⅛ tsp) salt

Graham Cracker Crust (Mix-In)

  • 30 g Arnott's Granita biscuits, crushed and frozen

Instructions

  1. Soften cream cheese in microwave for 10-15 seconds (should be soft but not melted)
  2. Add milk to high-sided container
  3. Add softened cream cheese and blend with immersion blender for 60 seconds until completely smooth (no lumps!)
  4. Add vanilla extract and True Lime packets, blend to fully dissolve
  5. Mix pudding mix, protein powder, erythritol, and salt in separate bowl
  6. Slowly add dry ingredients while blending with immersion blender
  7. Mix additional 90 seconds after all ingredients added
  8. Taste - should be very tangy and lime-forward
  9. Pour into pint, cap, freeze 24 hours
  10. Hot water rinse 60 seconds before processing
  11. Process on Light Ice Cream setting
  12. Make hole on side of pint
  13. Add frozen crushed Granita biscuits
  14. Run Mix-In setting
  15. Top with extra biscuit crumbs for presentation

Adelaide Shopping Guide

  • True Lime packets: iHerb.com.au, Amazon.com.au, some Chemist Warehouse
  • Arnott's Granita: Coles, Woolworths (biscuit aisle) - best graham cracker substitute
  • Reduced-fat cream cheese: Philadelphia Light (Coles, Woolworths)
  • Pudding mix: Aeroplane brand (Coles, Woolworths) or Jell-O (iHerb, USA Foods stores)
  • High protein milk: Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus

Pro Tips

  • Cream cheese MUST be smooth: Blend for full 60 seconds or you'll get lumps
  • Pudding mix adds body: Creates authentic key lime pie texture, skip xanthan gum when using pudding mix
  • True Lime is concentrated: 2 packets = juice of 2 whole limes without the liquid
  • Freeze graham crackers solid: Prevents them from getting soggy and pulverized
  • Cost per pint: ~$3.00 - still 60% cheaper than store-bought premium ice cream
  • Alternative crust: Use Digestive biscuits or Wheatmeal biscuits if Granita unavailable
