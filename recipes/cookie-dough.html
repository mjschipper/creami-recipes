Cookie Dough Ice Cream

Loaded with protein cookie dough chunks

Calories 320
Protein 54g
Carbs 28g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 430 ml 2% high protein milk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 32 g vanilla whey protein (1 scoop)
  • 10 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.5 g xanthan gum
  • 50 g cookie dough (see recipe)

Instructions

  1. Mix base using immersion blender method
  2. Freeze 24 hours
  3. Hot water rinse, spin on sorbet setting
  4. Make 2.5cm (1 inch) hole in center
  5. Add 50g cookie dough from fridge
  6. Cover with ice cream
  7. Run mix-in setting

Nutrition per Pint

320 Calories
54g Protein
28g Carbs
6g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Cookie dough makes 6 pints worth - refrigerate 24 hours for peak flavour
  • Dough recipe: 90g flour, 49g protein, 1.5g salt, 21g butter, 1 egg + yolk, vanilla, sweeteners, chocolate chips
