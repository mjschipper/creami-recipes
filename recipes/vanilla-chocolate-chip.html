Vanilla Bean Chocolate Chip

Classic vanilla bean with homemade chocolate chips

Calories 290
Protein 50g
Carbs 22g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 410 ml reduced fat high protein milk (A2 Protein Plus or Devondale Protein Plus)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/2 vanilla bean (seeds scraped)
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 16 g vanilla whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 8 g skim milk powder
  • 20 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.7 g xanthan gum
  • 26 g frozen chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Add milk, egg yolk, vanilla bean seeds, extract to container
  2. Mix dry ingredients
  3. Blend with immersion blender while adding dry ingredients
  4. Mix 90 seconds, let sit 10 minutes
  5. Freeze 24 hours
  6. Hot water rinse, spin on light ice cream
  7. Make hole in center, remove ice cream
  8. Add 26g frozen chocolate chips, push to bottom
  9. Cover with ice cream, run mix-in setting

Nutrition per Pint

290 Calories
50g Protein
22g Carbs
7g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Use same chocolate chip method as Mint Chocolate Chip
