Mint Chocolate Chip

Classic mint with homemade chocolate chips

Calories 260
Protein 50g
Carbs 22g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 430 ml light high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 8 drops green food colouring
  • 12 drops peppermint extract
  • 32 g vanilla whey protein (1 scoop)
  • 20 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.5 g xanthan gum
  • 30 g homemade chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Mix base using immersion blender method
  2. Freeze 24 hours
  3. Hot water rinse, spin on sorbet setting
  4. Make hole in center
  5. Quickly add 30g frozen chocolate chips - work fast!
  6. Push chips toward bottom
  7. Cover with ice cream
  8. Run mix-in setting immediately

Nutrition per Pint

260 Calories
50g Protein
22g Carbs
6g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Extract strength varies by brand - measure in DROPS
  • Make chocolate chips: melt 113g chocolate + 7g oil, spread thin, freeze, chop
