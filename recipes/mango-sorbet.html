Mango Sorbet

Tropical paradise - rich and intensely mango

Calories 110
Protein 1g
Carbs 27g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 280 g fresh ripe mango puree
  • 160 ml water
  • 15 g erythritol/Natvia (or 10g agave)
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 5 ml lime juice (optional)

Instructions

  1. Choose ripe mangoes with slight give when pressed
  2. Slice down both sides avoiding flat seed
  3. Score flesh in cross-hatch pattern while on skin
  4. Scoop out cubes with spoon
  5. Add 280g mango cubes to high-sided container
  6. Add 160g water, sweetener and salt (plus lime if using)
  7. Blend with immersion blender 90 seconds until smooth
  8. Taste - should be sweeter than desired
  9. Pour into pint, cap, freeze 24 hours
  10. Hot water rinse 60 seconds
  11. Spin on Sorbet, re-spin 2 times

Nutrition per Pint

110 Calories
1g Protein
27g Carbs
0g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Slightly overripe mangoes are perfect - sweeter and easier to blend
  • Best December-February when mangoes are peak season
