Fudge Brownie Ice Cream

Loaded with fudgy protein brownie chunks

Calories 350
Protein 50g
Carbs 28g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 410 ml light high protein milk (Farmers Union Supershake, Oak Plus, or Paul's Smarter White)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2.1 g vanilla extract
  • 6 drops coffee extract (optional)
  • 28 g chocolate whey protein (1 scoop)
  • 20 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 12 g cocoa powder (3 tbsp)
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 1 g xanthan gum
  • 56 g protein brownie chunks (see recipe)

Instructions

  1. Mix base ingredients using immersion blender method
  2. Freeze 24 hours
  3. Hot water rinse, spin on light ice cream
  4. Make hole near side of pint
  5. Add 44g frozen brownie chunks and push to bottom
  6. Add 12g more on side
  7. Cover with ice cream
  8. Run mix-in setting
  9. Top with extra brownie pieces

Nutrition per Pint

350 Calories
50g Protein
28g Carbs
8g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Make protein brownies separately - recipe makes 4 pints worth
  • Coffee extract enhances chocolate flavour without tasting like coffee
