Cantaloupe Sorbet

The dark horse flavour - surprisingly delicious

Calories 95
Protein 1g
Carbs 23g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 300 g fresh cantaloupe puree
  • 160 g water
  • 20 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt

Instructions

  1. Choose heavy cantaloupe with sweet smell at stem end
  2. Cut thin layer off both ends
  3. Cut in half, scoop out seeds
  4. Slice off thin rind
  5. Cut flesh into chunks, puree until smooth
  6. Add 300g cantaloupe puree to pint
  7. Add 160g water, erythritol and salt
  8. Blend 30 seconds
  9. Cap and freeze 24 hours
  10. Hot water rinse 60 seconds
  11. Spin on Sorbet, re-spin twice
  12. Level with spoon between re-spins

Nutrition per Pint

95 Calories
1g Protein
23g Carbs
0g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Many people don't expect to love this but it becomes a favourite
