All-Milk Vanilla Base

Improved texture and creaminess with an all-dairy base

Calories 180
Protein 25g
Carbs 16g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 440 ml 1% high protein milk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 45 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 1 g xanthan gum
  • 1 g salt

Instructions

  1. Add milk and vanilla to a high-sided container
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a separate bowl
  3. Blend wet ingredients with immersion blender
  4. Slowly add dry ingredients while blending
  5. Mix additional 90 seconds after all ingredients added
  6. Freeze for 24 hours
  7. Hot water rinse for 60 seconds, spin on light ice cream setting
  8. Re-spin or mix-in setting for final consistency

Nutrition per Pint

180 Calories
25g Protein
16g Carbs
3g Fat

Pro Tips

  • The all-dairy base significantly reduces ice crystals
  • Use high protein milk for best texture and protein content
