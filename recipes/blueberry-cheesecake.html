Blueberry Cheesecake Ice Cream

Antioxidant-rich with burst of blueberry flavour

Calories 325
Protein 44g
Carbs 28g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 380 g 1% high protein milk
  • 60 g fat-free cream cheese, softened
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 2.5 g lemon juice
  • 16 g vanilla whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 10 g fat-free milk powder
  • 35 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.7 g xanthan gum
  • 22 g graham cracker chunks
  • 30 g blueberry sauce

Instructions

  1. Make blueberry sauce: blend 150g blueberries, 20g erythritol, 5g lemon juice, heat and add 2g xanthan gum, cool and freeze
  2. Use New York Cheesecake base
  3. Follow same swirl technique as Strawberry Cheesecake
  4. Blueberries create a more dramatic purple swirl

Nutrition per Pint

325 Calories
44g Protein
28g Carbs
6g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Blueberries create beautiful purple swirls
