Rum Raisin Ice Cream

Classic sophisticated flavour - adults only!

Calories 310
Protein 44g
Carbs 32g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 400 ml reduced fat high protein milk (A2 Protein Plus or Devondale Protein Plus)
  • 20 g reserved rum liquid from raisins
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 16 g vanilla whey protein (1/2 scoop)
  • 10 g skim milk powder
  • 30 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 0.8 g xanthan gum
  • 40 g rum-soaked raisins

Instructions

  1. Prepare raisins: combine 40g raisins, 30g dark rum, 10g sugar, 2g cinnamon
  2. Microwave 30 seconds, stir, let soak minimum 2 hours (overnight best)
  3. Drain liquid (reserve it!), pat raisins dry
  4. Freeze raisins solid on parchment paper
  5. Make base with reserved rum liquid
  6. Freeze 24 hours
  7. Hot water rinse, spin on light ice cream
  8. Make hole off-center
  9. Add frozen rum-soaked raisins
  10. Push toward bottom, cover, run mix-in setting
  11. Top with few extra raisins

Nutrition per Pint

310 Calories
44g Protein
32g Carbs
4g Fat

Pro Tips

  • Soaking overnight gives maximum flavour
  • Bundaberg Rum is perfect for this! Local and delicious.
  • Non-alcoholic option: use rum extract only
