Mango Lassi Sorbet

Protein-boosted Indian classic - perfect for Adelaide summers

Calories 110
Protein 4g
Carbs 26g
Freeze Time 24 hours

Ingredients

  • 280 g fresh mango puree (about 1 large mango)
  • 140 g water
  • 20 g plain Greek yogurt
  • 25 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 2 g fresh lime juice
  • 1 g ground cardamom
  • 0.5 g salt
  • pinch saffron threads (optional)

Instructions

  1. Choose ripe mango with slight give when pressed
  2. Cut cheeks away from seed, score and scoop out cubes
  3. Add mango, water, yogurt, lime juice to container
  4. Add cardamom and saffron if using
  5. Add erythritol and salt
  6. Blend with immersion blender 90 seconds until completely smooth
  7. Taste - should be sweeter than desired
  8. Pour into pint, cap, freeze 24 hours
  9. Hot water rinse 60 seconds
  10. Spin on sorbet setting
  11. Re-spin twice, leveling between spins

Nutrition per Pint

110 Calories
4g Protein
26g Carbs
1g Fat

Pro Tips

  • The cardamom is essential - it's what makes it 'lassi' style!
  • Best when mangoes are in season (October-February in South Australia)
