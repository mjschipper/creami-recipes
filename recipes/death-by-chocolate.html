Death by Chocolate

Chocolate overload - base, chunks, chips, and brownie

Calories 380
Protein 48g
Carbs 30g
Freeze Time 36 hours

Ingredients

  • 380 ml 1% high protein milk
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5 ml vanilla extract
  • 6 drops coffee extract
  • 28 g chocolate whey protein (1 scoop)
  • 20 g erythritol/Natvia
  • 15 g cocoa powder (3.5 tbsp)
  • 0.5 g salt
  • 1 g xanthan gum
  • 14 g semi-sweet chocolate, melted
  • 20 g chocolate chips
  • 20 g brownie chunks
  • 10 g dark chocolate, chopped

Instructions

  1. Make base following Maximum Chocolate method
  2. Melt 14g chocolate, warm milk to 27-32°C (80-90°F)
  3. Blend thoroughly, mix in dry ingredients
  4. Freeze 36 hours (heated base)
  5. Hot water rinse, spin on sorbet setting
  6. Make large hole in center
  7. Layer mix-ins: chips, brownie, chocolate chunks, repeat
  8. Cover with ice cream
  9. Run mix-in setting
  10. Top with extra chocolate shavings

Nutrition per Pint

380 Calories
48g Protein
30g Carbs
12g Fat
